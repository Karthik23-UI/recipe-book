Chicken Noodles

Noodles are made from unleavened dough that is rolled flat and cut into long strips or threads before being stretched or extruded. It can be kept in the refrigerator for a short period of time or dried and stored for later use. Noodles are typically cooked in boiling water, with the addition of cooking oil or salt if desired. Noodles prepared from wheat dough became a popular dish among the Han dynasty's citizens. In China, the earliest evidence of noodles dates back 4,000 years.

Instructions

step 1

Ingredients and Preparation: Gather all the ingredients you'll need for chicken noodles, including chicken, noodles, vegetables, and sauces. Slice the chicken, mince garlic, and prepare any vegetables by slicing or julienning them.

step 2

Cook the Noodles: Boil a pot of water and add a pinch of salt.Add the egg noodles and stir immediately.Cook according to package instructions until they're tender but still slightly firm (al dente).Drain and set aside.

step 3

Cook the Chicken: Season the sliced chicken with salt and pepper. Heat a pan or wok over medium-high heat with vegetable oil. Add minced garlic and sliced chicken. Stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink (about 5-7 minutes).Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

step 4

Cook the Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed.Stir-fry the sliced vegetables (e.g., red bell pepper, carrot, broccoli) for about 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.

step 5

Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Add the cooked egg noodles to the pan.Drizzle with soy sauce and oyster sauce (optional).Toss everything together until well combined and heated through (about 2-3 minutes).Taste and adjust the seasoning with more soy sauce if needed.

Ingredients

8 oz (about 225g) of egg noodles

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 carrot, julienned

1 cup broccoli florets

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)

Salt and pepper to taste

Green onions, chopped for garnish (optional)

Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
